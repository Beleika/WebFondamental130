About PHOENIX

To become the leading service company to beautify the human being, while providing a harmonious, reciprocal and healthy work space for the human staff that responds to the needs of the guests. Raise the beauty market in the Dominican Republic by responding efficiently to the concerns and needs of our clients. Provide excellent beauty solutions and services in order to create valuable experiences for our guests. Offer a dynamic, enthusiastic and happy environment where comfort, quality and health are combined.

Value

Our greatest value is human capital, people, our Team. We offer high standards of quality and responsibility that allow us to reach our personal and group goals. Love, Service, Warranty, Quality, Innovation.
